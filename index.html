Acount selling

Epic games has made this attempt to stop account selling by bringing older skins back to the item shop. The attempt was succesfull, proven by the fact that not nearly as many people buy accounts with rare skins in their lockers in fear that they will return to the item shop. This attempt however, could go the other way. The owners of these rare accounts might relize that if the rare skins come back then his account will be worthless and because of that try even harder to sell his or her's account.
